Welcome to DeCasa Marble Sdn. Bhd. Your premier marble furnishing expert that brings the refined craft of marble bookmatching art to your home interior designs, in a wide range selection of exquisite European marble slabs and designs. Marble has been the building material for history’s most exulted architectures for its raw beauty and delicate patterns. With years of experience in the natural stone trade and craft, our skilled expertise are honed to perfect your home with ambiences of affluence from the natural elegance of timeless bookmatched marble designs. With firm and established operations in natural stone imports, DeCasa Marble Sdn. Bhd. also offers your home an extensive selection of various marbles and exquisite patterns that best compliments your interior design preferences. Our latest business partnership with international natural stone expert Cosentino, ensures that we offer you with innovative architectural and decorative solutions in natural stone that are of the upmost quality. Cosentino is the world’s premier provider of Silestone quartz surfaces, and is well-known amongst the top interior designers as an international brand that produces only the very best in designer natural stones. DeCasa Marble Sdn. Bhd., as one of the very few authorized dealers in Malaysia to provide exclusive products from Cosentino, is a testament to our reliability, responsibility and professional craftsmanship. Beyond consultation, design and installation, we also provide your completed living environments with expert marble maintenance services. Visit our showroom today for your exclusive consultation.
